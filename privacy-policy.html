At Prysm, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal and health information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Prysm collects and processes the following types of information:

  • Health Data: Heart rate, heart rate variability (HRV), respiratory rate, blood oxygen saturation (SpO2), wrist temperature, sleep data, activity data, steps, calories burned, and exercise minutes.
  • Personal Information: Account information such as email address if you create an account.
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and app version for technical support and optimization.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app, such as features accessed and session duration, to improve user experience.

2. How We Collect Information

We collect health data through Apple Health (HealthKit), which you must authorize when setting up the app. All data is sourced from your Apple Watch and iPhone's Health app. We only access data that you explicitly grant us permission to access.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide personalized health insights, recovery scores, sleep analysis, and biometric tracking
  • Calculate and display your biological age, stress levels, and energy bank status
  • Create personalized baselines and trends based on your historical data
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Provide customer support and respond to your inquiries
  • Send important updates about the app (if you've opted in to notifications)

4. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage: All health data is stored locally on your device. We do not upload your health data to external servers unless you explicitly opt-in to cloud backup features.

Apple Health Integration: Your data is managed through Apple's secure HealthKit framework, which provides industry-leading security and privacy protections.

Encryption: Any data transmitted to our servers (such as for account management or optional cloud features) is encrypted using industry-standard TLS/SSL encryption.

No Third-Party Sharing: We never sell, rent, or share your personal health data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: If you explicitly request data sharing with healthcare providers or other third-party services.
  • Service Providers: We may share non-health data with trusted service providers who help us operate the app (e.g., analytics, crash reporting), but they are contractually obligated to protect your data.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government regulation.
  • Business Transfers: If Prysm is acquired or merged with another company, your data may be transferred, but the new entity must honor this Privacy Policy.

6. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can access all your health data through Apple Health at any time.
  • Control: You can revoke Prysm's access to your health data at any time in iOS Settings > Privacy > Health > Prysm.
  • Deletion: You can delete your account and associated data by contacting prysmhealth@gmail.com.
  • Data Portability: You can export your data through Apple Health's built-in export functionality.

7. Children's Privacy

Prysm is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child, please contact us immediately.

8. Third-Party Services

Prysm integrates with Apple Health and may use third-party analytics services (e.g., for crash reporting). These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them:

  • Apple Privacy Policy

9. International Users

If you are using Prysm outside the United States, please be aware that your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States. By using the app, you consent to this transfer. We comply with applicable international data protection laws, including GDPR principles.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting a notice in the app or via email. Your continued use of Prysm after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: prysmhealth@gmail.com
Subject Line: Privacy Inquiry

12. HIPAA Compliance

While Prysm is designed with privacy best practices inspired by HIPAA regulations, please note that Prysm is a consumer wellness app and is not a HIPAA-covered entity. The app is not intended to replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

13. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected, used, shared, or sold
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (Note: We do not sell your data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at prysmhealth@gmail.com with "CCPA Request" in the subject line.
