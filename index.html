About Prysm

Prysm is a comprehensive health tracking app that integrates seamlessly with Apple Health and Apple Watch to provide personalized insights on your recovery, sleep quality, stress levels, energy management, and overall wellness. Using advanced algorithms and your personalized health baselines, Prysm helps you understand your body better and optimize your health every day.

Whether you're an athlete looking to optimize performance, a health enthusiast tracking wellness metrics, or someone simply wanting to understand their body better, Prysm provides the insights you need to make informed decisions about your health.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect my Apple Watch?

Prysm automatically syncs with Apple Health. Simply ensure your Apple Watch is paired with your iPhone and that you've granted Prysm permission to access Apple Health data. The app will automatically pull data from your watch through Apple Health.

What health metrics does the app track?

Prysm tracks a comprehensive set of biometrics including Heart Rate Variability (HRV), resting heart rate, current heart rate, respiratory rate, blood oxygen saturation (SpO2), wrist temperature, sleep duration and quality, active calories, steps, exercise minutes, and more. All data is sourced from Apple Health.

How is my data protected?

Your privacy is our top priority. All health data is stored locally on your device and synced through Apple Health's secure infrastructure. We never sell your data to third parties, and any cloud features use end-to-end encryption. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

What are the subscription options?

Prysm offers a free 14-day trial so you can explore all features. After the trial, you can subscribe to unlock premium features including advanced analytics, historical trends, premium widgets, and personalized insights. Subscription pricing is available in the app.

How do I interpret my recovery score?

Your recovery score (0-100) indicates how well your body has recovered and how ready you are for physical or mental stress. Scores above 67 indicate good recovery, 33-67 suggest moderate recovery, and below 33 means you should prioritize rest. The score is personalized based on your unique baselines.

Why isn't my data syncing?

First, ensure Apple Health permissions are granted in Settings > Privacy > Health > Prysm. Make sure your Apple Watch is connected and syncing with your iPhone. Try force-closing and reopening the app. If issues persist, check that you're wearing your Apple Watch properly and that it's updated to the latest watchOS version.

How do I reset my baselines?

Baselines are automatically calculated based on your historical data (typically 7-30 days). As you use the app more, baselines become more accurate. If you'd like to manually reset baselines due to significant lifestyle changes, contact support and we can assist you.

Can I export my data?

All your health data remains accessible through Apple Health, which has built-in export functionality. You can export your data from Apple Health at any time. We're working on adding direct export features within Prysm for easier access to your insights.

What devices are supported?

Prysm requires an iPhone running iOS 16 or later. For the best experience with real-time biometric tracking, we recommend using an Apple Watch Series 4 or newer. Some features may have limited functionality without an Apple Watch.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through the App Store. To cancel: Open Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions > Prysm > Cancel Subscription. You'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.
