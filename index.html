Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about using Prysm and understanding your health data.

How do I connect my Apple Watch?

Prysm automatically syncs with Apple Health. Simply ensure your Apple Watch is paired with your iPhone and grant Prysm permission to access Health data in Settings.

What metrics does Prysm track?

We track HRV, heart rate, respiratory rate, blood oxygen (SpO2), wrist temperature, sleep duration and quality, activity levels, steps, calories, and more through Apple Health.

How do I interpret my recovery score?

Recovery scores range from 0-100. Scores above 67 indicate good recovery, 33-67 suggest moderate recovery, and below 33 means prioritize rest. Scores are personalized to your baseline.

Why isn't my data syncing?

Ensure Apple Health permissions are granted in Settings > Privacy > Health > Prysm. Check that your Apple Watch is connected and syncing with your iPhone.

What devices are supported?

Prysm requires iOS 16 or later. For optimal biometric tracking, we recommend Apple Watch Series 4 or newer.

How do I manage my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through the App Store. Go to Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions > Prysm to view or cancel.
